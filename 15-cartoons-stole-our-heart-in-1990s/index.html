15 CARTOONS STOLE OUR HEART IN 1990’S

By Vashishta Pochampally Last updated Apr 16, 2018
0
Share

Cartoons, a major part of our childhood. Cartoons always get kids attention with the colorful characters, weird characters, funny sounds. Cartoons always made us laugh and some of us still watch cartoons.

Here are some cartoons from 90’s which stole our hearts.

1.TOM AND JERRY

Tom and Jerry is one the our favorite cartoons of our childhood, which inspired us to fight all the time with our siblings and friends.

2.POPEYE

Popeye the sailor man..tring..tring. This cartoon actually made most of us believe that spinach can give us super powers.

3.COURAGE THE COWARDLY DOG

This cartoon scared the hell out of us. This cartoon is scarier than actual horror movies.

4.DEXTER’S LABORATORY

This little Dexter made us all fall in love with science.

5.HE-MAN

We all made a wooden sword to turn into He-man and pretended to be like him.

6.SCOOBY DOO

This show actually made us investigate our neighbourhood with our friends.

7.LOONEY TOONS

The most loved cartoons we could enjoy on Mute.

8.SIMPSONS

The Simpsons had depicted Donald Trump running for President back in 2000.

9.SPIDERMAN

Spider man was always one of our top favorites. He followed us for another decade with movies.

10.TALESPIN

Bhaloo from Talespin, who made us dream of flying a plane one day when we grew up.

11.ALADDIN

We searched for that secret lamp with Genie in it, to make our wishes come true.

12.BOB THE BUILDER

Bob the builder, the man who inspired us to build pillow castles. Bob the builder is one of the brilliant cartoons ever.

    13.DOUG

The life of a young lad writes about his love, his friends and how he maneuver his way through 6th grade.

14.THE REN & STIMPY SHOW

The gross misadventures of a hyper chihuahua and a stupid cat. It is one of the most entertaining cartoons ever.

15.BATMAN: THE ANIMATED SERIES

Batman is worlds favorite super hero. Not only kids, even adults love to watch this cartoon. This animated series of batman is very inspirational.
